About Naija Soccer Stars

Home / About us

Naija Soccer Stars is a Reality TV Show initiated by Kindness Guild Nigeria (KGN) in Partnership with Alphagrace Media Resources Ltd (Concept, Marketing Strategy and Execution). It is designed to help millions of Nigerian youths who desires a future in football to find and take their place in soccer stardom. By making it into a Reality Show, NAIJA SOCCER Stars hopes to expose its participants to local and international observers, scouts, football clubs with in and outside Nigeria and even Corporate Brands. We believe that there is the star factor in everyone and finding, nurturing and exposing it to the world is why we are tick. Welcome to STARDOM
