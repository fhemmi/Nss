4. How can I get the sendmenaija application

Ans: Visit Google play store and type in sendmenaija and download.

Ans: Go to Events and Tickets. Click on Naija Soccer Stars Tv Reality Show and continue to purchase

Ans: You will have the amount deducted from your wallet or your bank account. Plus you will receive an e-ticket which you will Then print and bring to the screening venue.

Ans: Make sure you purchase the form one at a time. When you get to shipping address, instead of clicking your own address, input the address and email and other information belonging to the person you are buying for and ask him to check his inbox for the e-ticket.
